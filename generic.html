me & my passion

With the explosion of machine learning and artificial intelligence, how can we apply breakthroughs in electrical engineering and computer science (EECS) to other fields? From research to solutions to real-world problems, how can I help more people benefit from cutting-edge technology? Inspired by these questions, I conducted several interdisciplinary research projects at SUSTech and UC Berkeley. By applying EECS to different domains such as Design Optimization, Education, Swarm Robotics, and Mesh Networks, I developed a wide range of skills along with a passion for EECS.
