Greetings! I’m Yunzhong, many know me as Shawn. I’m pursuing my Master's degree in Mobile and IoT Engineering at CMU and looking forward to graduating in December 2024. Now I'm actively exploring SDE/MLE internship opportunities for summer 2024.
I am a tech enthusiast fervent for optimizing the bridge between software and the physical world. Having worked intensively with both software and hardware, I've co-developed VR environments for Intelligent Tutoring Systems, optimized chip packaging designs using Bayesian algorithms, and led teams in designing marine data collection vehicles. My penchant for innovation extends into individual endeavors, such as the creation of ChatYTB, an LLM-powered application designed to efficiently summarize and chat with YouTube video content.
While my technical skillset includes languages like Python, Java, C, and C#, and tools such as AWS, Docker, Git, and Flask, I truly believe in the synergy of collective efforts and cross-disciplinary collaboration. A testament to this is my leadership at Berkeley, where I spearheaded a team of 10 individuals from 6 different countries on a project focused on Unmanned Surface Vehicles Swarm.
On a lighter note, when not engrossed in the tech world, I enjoy capturing the world from above with my drone — that is, when I manage to keep it airborne! :)
